Key Regional Terminology

Momo
A type of steamed dumpling, highly popular in the hills of Shimla and Shillong.
Chai
Spiced, sweetened tea, a universal drink across all Indian cities and towns.
Ghat
The series of steps leading to a river or tank, prominently seen in Varanasi and Udaipur.
